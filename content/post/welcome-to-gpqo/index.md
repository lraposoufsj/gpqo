Welcome to the new GPQO website! We have built this space to share our research,
introduce our group members, and keep the community updated on our latest news and publications.

## What you will find here

- **People** — meet the principal investigators, students, and alumni of the group
- **Research** — an overview of our current projects and research lines
- **Publications** — our full publication record, updated regularly
- **Resources** — templates and tools for group members

We will be posting updates here regularly — new publications, group achievements,
conference presentations, and occasional longer posts on topics in organic chemistry.

Stay tuned!
