abstract: Bench-stable tablets (COtabs) have been developed for the rapid and safe
  production of carbon monoxide. The tablets can be made in less than 5 min without
  the use of a glovebox and only require a stock solution of an amine base to liberate
  a specific quantity of CO in a two-chamber system. The COtabs were tested in five
  different carbonylation reactions and provided similar yields compared to literature
  procedures. Finally, a gram-scale reaction was conducted, as well as 13C-isotope
  labeling of the anticancer drug, olaparib.
