  and indolizidines were accomplished from a common chiral endocyclic enecarbamate.

  endocyclic enecarbamate and a highly regioselective Baeyer--Villiger oxidation of
  the intermediate azabicyclic-cyclobutanones. These new heterocycles are compounds
  structurally related to the alexines.
